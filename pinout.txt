Pinout from Raspberry Pi to Expansion Board and peripherals 
    RPi   ------  EB
GND             - GND
GPIO2 (SDA)     - SDA 
GPIO3 (SCL)     - SCL 
GPIO4 (1w)      - DHT22_1 
GPIO17 (1w)     - DHT22_2 
GPIO 22,23,24   - 1x3_relay (pumps 1-3)
GPIO 18,27      - 1x2_relay (grow LED lights and camera lights)  
GPIO 9          - pushbutton_1 (manual camera capture) 
GPIO 10         - pushbutton_2 (manual pump watering) 
GPIO 25         - LED (heartbeat)
